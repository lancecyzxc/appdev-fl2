Sports Games

These games simulate real-world sports. Examples include football games, basketball games, and baseball games.

Simulation Games

These games simulate real-world activities, such as driving, flying, or farming. Examples include racing games, flight simulators, and city-building games.

Role-playing games (RPGs)

In RPGs, you take on the role of a character and explore a virtual world. You can level up your character, learn new skills, and complete quests. Examples include MMORPGs (massively multiplayer online RPGs) and JRPGs (Japanese RPGs).
